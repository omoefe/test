About Us

We screen and recruit the most qualified and competent seafarers and officers through a meticulous and tested system. Before placing any crew on your vessel, Marine Crewing Services will:

  • Meticulously check the prospective crewmembers’ previous experience and performance, including a review of their prior service records and a character reference from previous employers or agencies.
  • Ensure that they possess all valid documentation required by the vessel’s flag state and the countries where they intend to work.
  • Provide the candidates with proper orientation about duties, responsibilities and expectations while on-board the ship.
  • Furnish ship owners/principals with all records pertaining to their deployed crewmembers, such as medical exams reports, license and certifications, passport and visa information, employment contracts, allotment slips, payrolls, etc.
  • All transportation arrangements for placed crewmembers are made through our sister company, Marine Travel Services.
